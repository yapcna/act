Birds of a Feather Sessions

Birds of a Feather (BoF) sessions provide face to face exposure to those interested in the same projects and concepts. BoFs can be organized for individual projects or broader topics (best practices, open data, standards). BoFs are entirely up to you: you provide the engaging topic. Share your topic on the wiki, then we'll promote it at the conference and help you schedule space and time.

Scheduling

Space and a notice on the official schedule will be allocated during the conference for people who would like to hold BoF sessions. Spaces will be allocated in a first-come first-serve manner. Please contact us at hackathon@yapcna.org if you would like help scheduling your BoF. Include the topic, synopsis, and estimated length of the BoF session for scheduling purposes.
